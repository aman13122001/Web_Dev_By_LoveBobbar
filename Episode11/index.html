about Codehelp

CodeHelp is a platform and initiative that provides curated educational resources, primarily focused on coding, for students and developers. It aims to help individuals learn and improve their coding skills through various resources like documentations, practice problems, and project ideas, all delivered in a concise and practical manner. CodeHelp emphasizes hands-on learning through interactive labs and challenges, helping users escape the trap of traditional tutorial-based learning
